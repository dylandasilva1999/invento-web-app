Our Story

Invento was founded by Tim Burton in 2021, alongside both Daniel Craigs and Natasha Filly as co- founders. Invento is a specified interactive web application Minecraft inventory tracker for those crafter enthusiasts, who want to bring their game to another level, enhancing their technical capabilities with ease.
